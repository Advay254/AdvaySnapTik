How to Download Slideshow Images

  1. Open TikTok, find the photo slideshow post, tap Share → Copy Link.
  2. Go to advaysnaptik.onrender.com and paste the URL.
  3. Tap Get Download Links.
  4. Switch to the Slideshow tab in the result card.
  5. Tap any thumbnail to download that image, or tap Download All to save every photo.

What Format Are the Images?

Images are downloaded as JPEG files at the original resolution TikTok stores them — typically full-quality photos as uploaded by the creator.

How Do I Know If a TikTok Is a Slideshow?

Slideshow posts show multiple images you can swipe through. When you paste a slideshow link into AdvaySnapTik, the Slideshow tab will automatically activate and show all the images.
